Homemade Everything Bagel

An everything bagel is a type of bagel baked with a mix of toppings. The exact ingredients vary, but recipes may include caraway seeds, garlic flakes, onion flakes, poppy seeds, sesame seeds and salt. The bagels are made with regular dough and the name is independent of additional fillings such as cream cheese.

Raspberry Cheesecake

A raspberry cheesecake is a delicious dessert that is made with a creamy cheesecake base and topped with a layer of sweet and tangy raspberry sauce. The cheesecake base is typically made with cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and heavy cream, and is baked in a pie crust or a graham cracker crust. The raspberry sauce is made with fresh or frozen raspberries, sugar, and a small amount of cornstarch to thicken the sauce.
