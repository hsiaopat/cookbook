Walnut Katayif

PREP: 1 hour

Cook: 30 min

Servings: 24

Rating: 5/5

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Fine Semolina
  • 1 Cup All Prupose Flour
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Yeast
  • 1 Tsp Baking Power
  • 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 Tbsp Orange Blossom Water
  • 3 Tbsp Sugar
  • 3 Tbsp Dry milk
  • 3 1/2 Cups Water

Filling

  • 2 Cups Crushed walnuts
  • 1 Cup Shredded coconut
  • 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Cinnamon
  • 1/4 Tsp Nutmeg

Instructions
