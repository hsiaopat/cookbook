Who Are We?


We are three third-year students studying at the Culinary Institute of America in New York City! Our love for food began at an early age, with all of us starting to help our parents cook back in elementary school. As the years progressed, we became more and more enchanted with the magic that comes alive in the kitchen when food is being prepared. Now, as we learn all types of new techniques in school and are exposed to an incredibly diverse cuisine in the Big Apple, we've been inspired to start a food blog of our own. Here, we will share some of our favorite recipes, restaurants, and more. Join us on the ride!
